Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how Individualuri Metsarme Aleksandr Zaitsev ("we", "us", "our"), an Individual Entrepreneur registered in Georgia (ID: 345702536), collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the Что ей ответить? service ("Service") at avotkaknado.space.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the Service:

  • Images you upload — screenshots of messaging conversations that you voluntarily submit for analysis. These are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after the response is generated.
  • Text context — any additional description you type into the context field. Treated the same as uploaded images — processed and discarded.
  • Usage data — anonymous request counts stored locally in your browser to manage the free trial limit. This data does not leave your device.
  • Payment data — if you make a purchase, payment information is collected and processed exclusively by Paddle.com. We do not receive or store your card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

The images and text you provide are used solely to generate AI-powered message suggestions via the Anthropic Claude API. We do not use your data to train AI models, build user profiles, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

Uploaded screenshots and text context are not stored after processing. Once the AI response is returned to you, your input data is discarded. We do not maintain a database of user conversations.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Anthropic Claude API — processes your uploaded content to generate message suggestions. Subject to Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Paddle.com — handles all payment processing as Merchant of Record. Subject to Paddle's privacy policy at paddle.com/legal/privacy.
  • Vercel — hosts the Service infrastructure. Subject to Vercel's privacy policy at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

6. Cookies

The Service uses minimal browser storage (localStorage) only to track the number of free requests used. No tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics scripts are used.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data beyond payment records handled by Paddle, there is no user data to request, correct, or delete on our end. For questions about payment data, contact Paddle directly at paddle.com.

If you have any privacy concerns, you may contact us at support@avotkaknado.space and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
