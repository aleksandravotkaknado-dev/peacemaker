Refund Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Overview

We want you to be satisfied with the Service. This policy explains when refunds are available and how to request one. All payments are processed by Paddle.com, who acts as our Merchant of Record.

Refund Eligibility

Eligible for refund
  • You were charged but the Service was unavailable and you could not use your requests
  • A technical error caused a duplicate charge
  • You requested a refund within 14 days of purchase and have not used any paid requests from that purchase
  • Subscription renewal was charged after you had already cancelled, due to a technical error
Reviewed case by case
  • You purchased a request pack but used only some of the requests and are unsatisfied with the quality of results
  • Monthly subscription — refund requested within 3 days of billing with minimal usage
Not eligible for refund
  • Requests were used and AI responses were delivered, regardless of whether the suggested messages achieved the desired relationship outcome
  • Refund requested more than 30 days after purchase
  • Subscription cancelled mid-period — access continues until the end of the paid period, no partial refund is issued
  • Dissatisfaction with AI-generated content style or tone after requests have been used

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@avotkaknado.space with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • The email address used for purchase
  • The date of purchase
  • A brief description of the reason for your request

We will review your request and respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed by Paddle.com and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future billing immediately. Access to the Service continues until the end of the current paid period. No partial refunds are issued for unused days in a cancelled subscription period unless required by applicable law.

Paddle Buyer Protection

Since Paddle.com is the Merchant of Record for all transactions, you may also be eligible for Paddle's own buyer protection. If we are unable to resolve your issue, you can contact Paddle support directly at paddle.com/support.
